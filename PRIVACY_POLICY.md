# Privacy Policy for All for Markdown

**Last Updated: December 13, 2024**

## Overview

All for Markdown ("the App") is a personal productivity application that helps users track daily activities and generate Markdown-formatted logs. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

### Data We Collect
- **Activity Data**: The App stores data you enter (tasks, habits, expenses, mood, etc.) locally on your device.
- **Health Data**: If you enable Health Connect integration, the App accesses step count, heart rate, and other health metrics with your explicit permission.

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
- We do not track your location.
- We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs.
- We do not transmit your data to external servers.

## Data Storage

All data is stored **locally on your device** using SQLite database. Your data is not uploaded to any cloud service unless you explicitly choose to export it.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following permissions:
- **Health Connect (Android)**: To sync health data from your device. This data remains on your device.

## Data Export

You can export your data as Markdown files at any time. These files are saved to a location you choose on your device.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time through the Settings screen in the App.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
